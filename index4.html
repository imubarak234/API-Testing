Who we are

Formed in February 2013, the party is the result of a merger of Nigeria's three biggest opposition parties - the Action Congress of Nigeria (ACN), the Congress for Progrssive Change (CPC), the All Nigeria Peoples Party (ANPP), a faction of the All Progressive Grand Alliance (APGA) and the new PDP - a faction of the then ruling People's Democratic Party. The resolution was signed by Tom Ikimi who represented the ACN; Senator Annie Okonkwo on half of the APGA; Ibrahim Shekarau, the Chairman of ANPP's Merger Committee; and the Chairman of CPC's Merger Committee.
